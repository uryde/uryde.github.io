Features

uRyde connects college students who lack transportation. Whether you are offering or requesting rides, uRyde makes it easy to find your way home.

Offers

Making offers provides students the ability to give others a friendly ride home, and a great opportunity to network with them while attending university.

Requests

Request a ride from another student heading in the same direction. Posting a request allows students to let you know when they are going home, and are willing to give you a ride.

Notifications

Recieve notifications from other students who make offers or request rides from you. Upon acceptance, you are instantly connected with them in order to negotiate.
